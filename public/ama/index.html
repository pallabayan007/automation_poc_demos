About Golden Bridge Solution
Golden Bridge is the “nervous system” of Automation Platform that senses a problem in an IT ecosystem and “gets it resolved” through backend Robotic Automation

  • Golden Bridge leverages Cognitive Intelligence of Watson (the Brain) to disambiguate a complex problem
  • It also enables scheduled automation for periodic health monitoring and maintenance activities
  • Golden Bridge is appropriate where enterprise events ( problem, incidents, alerts, work orders, service requests, conversational dialogs, web portal or scheduler) are intercepted and remediated through RPA tools
  • Look at Golden Bridge, when you have events, and automation tool, but no visible connection
  • Think of Golden Bridge when problems to be addressed through conversation chat-bot
  • Offers orchestration for complex automation in hybrid automation ecosystem. Any complex business problem may be segregated into smaller problems and orchestrated within Golden Bridge
  • Adapter based framework supports various industry standard protocols for integration
Enables End to End Automation in IT Operations
  • Golden Bridge integrates a range of IT service management systems (ITSM) with market leading RPA tools to remediate business problems
  • Golden bridge supports loosely coupled integration to add new ITSM tool or automation tool easily
Optimizes Utilization of Robot
Golden Bridge intercepts monitoring alerts, and helps optimizing Robot’s usage
  • Instead of monitoring for an alert, Robot would be free, to work on other items and get engaged only when a remediation is needed.
  • In a typical IT operations scenario, Robot needs to work only five minutes, five times a day, instead of 24x7 monitoring
Enables Intelligent Automation Through Cognitive – Robotic Integration
  • Golden Bridge intercepts cognitive conversation requests and invokes appropriate Robots to get things done, in a near real time asynchronous integration.
Orchestration Of SOPs - Complex and Human Intervention
  • Golden Bridge offers to attach multiple Standard Operations Procedures (SOP) in a choreographed sequence, to execute a specific task or group of tasks
  • Golden Bridge allows human intervention during the orchestration execution
